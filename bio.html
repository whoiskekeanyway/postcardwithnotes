Seun O Ogundele
Visual Storyteller

I am a visual storyteller and front-end developer, I love telling stories from my perspective with connection to where and how I grew up, I like documenting culture, capture the world or everyday life, as it exists, furthermore, my love for patterns, shapes and beautiful details led me into photography, I was a big lover of the art of photography before I became photograher.

I am very organised, attentive to detail and mostly self motivated. Above all, I am result oriented, and my modus operandi is simply paying attention to every detail, and leaving no stone unturned. I am reliable and very resourceful.

I am an avid learner, I believe that acquired knowledge is never a waste, I am also a big advocate of learning by doing, I am creatively curious, always open to indulge in it my my curiosity. Researching to understand and learn about what is happening around me has also improved my work in the past few years

I enjoy photography so much because I'm able to bring barely seen angles, from very familiar objects. My personal desire to witness and better understand the world around me has also helped me deliver some beautiful projects
